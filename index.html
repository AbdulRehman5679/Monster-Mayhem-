Game Rules

1. Each player takes turns placing a monster (V for Vampire, W for Werewolf, G for Ghost).

2. A player can place a monster on any unoccupied cell on their side of the grid.

3. Monsters can move any number of squares horizontally or vertically, or up to two squares diagonally.

4. Conflicts between monsters are resolved as follows:

  • If a Vampire (V) and a Werewolf (W) meet, the Werewolf is removed.
  • If a Werewolf (W) and a Ghost (G) meet, the Ghost is removed.
  • If a Ghost (G) and a Vampire (V) meet, the Vampire is removed.
  • If two monsters of the same type meet, both are removed.

5. A player is eliminated once 10 of their monsters are removed.

6. The game ends when all other players are eliminated.

Game Tutorial

Welcome to Monster Mayhem! Here's how to play:

  • Start the game by clicking the "Start Game" button.
  • Each player takes turns to place a monster (V for Vampire, W for Werewolf, G for Ghost) on the grid.
  • Click on any empty cell and enter the monster type when prompted.
  • Monsters can move to any unoccupied cell horizontally, vertically, or up to two squares diagonally.
  • When two monsters meet, conflicts are resolved based on the following rules:
    • If a Vampire (V) and a Werewolf (W) meet, the Werewolf is removed.
    • If a Werewolf (W) and a Ghost (G) meet, the Ghost is removed.
    • If a Ghost (G) and a Vampire (V) meet, the Vampire is removed.
    • If two monsters of the same type meet, both are removed.
  • The game continues until a player loses all 10 of their monsters.
  • The last player remaining wins the game!
